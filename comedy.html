The fun starts this Staurday at 7:15 P.M.!


It's all in the name. Come join us for some laughs as we host a full line up of the hottest comedians in the country. Featuring Jay Davis, Dane Cook and more comedians to be announced!

Jay Davis

Background: Jay Davis is an American actor, stand up comedian, and comedy promoter/producer who garnered nationwide attention after appearing in Dane Cook's Tourgasm, a 30-day cross-country tour turned reality show which also included Cook, Gary Gulman, and Robert Kelly, and was given a seven episode run on HBO.

Davis continues to perform stand up comedy throughout the United States, both with and without his Tourgasm colleagues. In addition, he books, produces, and hosts the long-running Life of the Party live stand up comedy show, Tuesday nights at the Laugh Factory in Hollywood, California.

Dane Cook

Background: Named a comedian phenomenon and icon by Entertainment Weekly, Dane Cook has maintained his reputation as one of today's most prolific stand up comedians, while simultaneously distinguishing himself as a charismatic and versatile actor in a variety of film and television projects. Cook created, directed, and starred in the network's nine part comedy documentary series, Tourgasm, and wrote, executive produced, and starred in Dane Cook: Vicious Circle, a feature length stand up event.

Cook currently lives in Los Angeles, where he continues to develop new comedy material, and film and television projects.
