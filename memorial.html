What are we up to?
Our goal is to raise $250,000 to fund a WWI centennial memorial, the first such memorial in over 100 years, at the Los Angles National Cemetery. This will feature Sgt. Henry Johnson honoring all the valor of the war. There is a scale model of the bronze memorial.
